Louisiana's Comprehensive Plans

The map below is an attempt to catalog all of the comprehensive plans that currently exist (or are under development) throughout Louisiana as of 2013. One layer identifies Parish comprehensive plans, the second identifies municipal or City comprehensive plans Obviously, Baton Rouge and New Orleans have combined city-parish comprehensive plans. The map links to a Google Fusion Table containing specifics about the plan and corresponding website links. Also, I've collected as many digital copies of the plans as I can find and storing them in a Google Drive account.

  • Grey = no comprehensive plan
  • Green = comprehensive plan exists
  • Blue = comprehensive plan final draft complete but not adopted
  • Red = acomprehensive plan process is underway






The data points illustrated on these maps is maintained in Google Fusion Tables and is property of Franklin Associates, LLC.
